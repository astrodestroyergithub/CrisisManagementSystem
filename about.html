Crisis Management is an initiative towards providing relief to needy people in times of crisis and chaos. Through this Web Dev project we aim to address the problem of all sorts (natural calamity, economic crisis, death-threats, kidnapping, epidemic, etc.) to the people who need them in the times of utmost distress, grief and pain. It resolves the aforementioned issues in a technical and data-driven manner and tries to establish the feeling of contentment.
